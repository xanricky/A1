About CossiePlay

CossiePlay is a family-run business in HulksTown since 2012. We create custom cosplay and fancy dress costumes and run a monthly cosplay party.Cosplayers can order any kind of costumes. Depending on difficulty, costumes take between 1 and 50 weeks and cost between $10 and $100. Dressing up is fun, good for the socially awkward and very creative.
